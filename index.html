about

Bloom is a cozy journaling app that turns your day into a flower. You pick how you are feeling, write your memory, add a song, and optionally add a photo. As you add more, your flower grows from Seed to Sapling to Bloom.

Over time, all your days collect into a Garden you can tap through to revisit past entries. The long term goal is to collect every flower, and it is not easy. Bloom is meant to feel simple, gentle, and a little game-like, so showing up each day feels natural.

privacy policy

what we collect

  • purchases: Apple (and RevenueCat) handle payments. We only get a confirmation so premium unlocks.
  • photos: only photos you add to a journal entry are stored locally on your device. we do not upload, read, or analyze them unless you explicitly add them.

what we don't collect

  • your entries: they stay on your device. We don't read, upload, or sell them.
  • personal info, trackers, or ads: none.

backups & deletes

Backups are left to iCloud if you enable them. Deleting the app removes local data from that device. If you use iCloud Backup, an iCloud restore could bring old data back — to fully remove, delete the app and remove the device backup in iCloud settings.
